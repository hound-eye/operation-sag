Russian 1st MSV (Motor Rifle Troops) Battalion of the 58th MSV Brigade is the unit that is deployed in this area.
The battalion consists primarily of mobilized conscripts, with some volunteer/contracted servicemen (mostly officers). As the result of heavy losses sustained during its deployment, it is severely understaffed, underequipped, and maintains poor discipline.

1st Battalion structure:


- 3 MSV rifle companies - that are spread across the village and its surrounding area. They also man the outposts and conduct patrols on foot or light unarmored vehicles.
- Mortar battery - equipped with 2B14 Podnos mortars.
- Anti-air platoon - a team that employs IGLA MANPADS and truck-mounted ZU-23 cannons for defense against aircraft and drones.
- Signal platoon - responsible for manning the R-142N vehicle, equipped for long-range communications.
- Airreconnoisance platoon - a scout team, that uses TIGR armored vehicles and employs various drones.
- Support and maintenance platoon - supporting unit that provides maintenance for the battalion's weapons, vehicles, and other provisions.

Outposts


Our intel counted at least 2 fortified checkpoints at the western and eastern approaches to the Vasylivka. They a garrisoned by well-equipped troops, and at least 1 crew-serverd machine gun. We presume there could be a small amount of supplies (weapons, ammunition, medical resources) stashed at these outposts.

Fighting vehicles and weapon systems


A large amount of this battalion's force, including its fighting vehicles (BTRs and BMPs) is currently not present in this area, as it is involved in another ongoing operation at the frontline. However, we can expect neighboring enemy units will send their own mechanized and motorized troops to the assistance of the Vasylivka garrison.
