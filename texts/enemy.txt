Russian 1st MSV (Motor rifle troops) Battalion of the 58th MSV Brigade is the unit that is deployed in this area.

The battalion consists primarily of mobilized conscripts, with some volunteer soldiers (mostly officers).
And as the result of heavy losses sustained during its deployment, it is severely understaffed and underequipped, and maintains a poor discipline.

Enemy battalion consists of:
- 3 motor rifle companies, that are spread across the village and its surrounding area. They also man the outposts and conduct patrols.
- Mortar battery - equipped with Podnos mortars.
- Anti-air platoon - team that employs IGLA MANPADS and one truck-mounted ZU-23 cannon for defense against aircraft & drones.
- Signal platoon - responsible for manning the R-142N communications vehicle.
- Airreconnoisance platoon - a scout team, that uses TIGR armored vehicle and employs various drones.
- Supply & maintenance platoon - supporting unit that provides maintenance for battalion's weapons, vehicle and other provisions.


All troops are using standard issue small arms, such as AK-74, SVD, PKP. Individual protection (Body Armor) is rarely worn
